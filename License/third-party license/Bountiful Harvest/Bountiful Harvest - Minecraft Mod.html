Bountiful Harvest is a thanksgiving themed addon for Farmer's Delight that seeks to add traditional Thanksgiving meals and items to Minecraft, alongside some other nice additions.

Additions

  • 3 Brand new crops (more planned)
  • A brand new mob, the Turkey!
  • New Feasts
  • A new decorational item (more planned)

Q&A

Q: 1.20?

A: I do not plan to support any older versions at this time.

Q: Fabric?

A: No.

Q: Can I use this in my Modpack?

A: Of course, just keep it within the Modrinth Ecosystem.
